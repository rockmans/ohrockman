Minutes for Saturday, July 28, 2012

On Saturday, July 28, 2012 the 37th annual meeting of the Rockman Reunion was called to order by our President, Mary Mertinke, at River Park in Mosinee. A moment of silence was observed for those that have passed on from us.


A total of 64 members attended the meeting, of which 40 where adults.


Kay Lamb gave the treasurers report:
- Last year we ended with a balance of $360.77
- $83.50 was spent on the cemetery beautification, on the graves of our loved ones
- Current balance of $277.27 in the general fund.

- Current balance of $633.18 in the savings account.
- Larry and Marylou Rockman donated $10.00 to the cemetery fund.

- The Virginia Miller Knights family donated $100.00 to be put in the general fund and used as needed.

Mark Brilowski made a motion to accept the treasures report and Rick Miller seconded it.


Meeting minutes from last year were e-mailed out and posted on the family website.


Larry Rockman said no money was needed for the website upkeep. He said we are good for a couple of years yet. Larry said the website address is: www.ohrockman.com and you can e-mail Larry at webmaster@ohrockman.com

Larry asked everyone to please, send more information for the website and asked us all to look over want is already on the website to make sure it is correct.


Nancy Houghtby asked if we will continue to have this reunion at River Park. Ken Rieck, who has for a number of years taken care of securing the park for us, has already reserved the park for 2013. Lynn input that the playground and bathrooms are a long way away, and maybe we should look at other options like Desert Park.


Nancy Houghtby reported that she found a cousin of ours on Face book. His name is Ulf Leadvikson. Nancy said he speaks good English and he told Nancy to say “hi” to all of us. He lives in Pranheim, Norway.


Mary again mentioned that we have River Park for the entire day. She asked everyone what they thought about staying longer at the park. Mary and Kay Lamb will be discussing this topic and will come up with some fun and interesting things for us to do next year. Mary is going to work on a Rockman trivia sheet for us to do. Hopefully, everyone will plan to stay longer next year and get to know each other better.


Jerry mentioned that years ago the kids would be taken to another place to play games so the meeting could go on and adults talk. Some discussion on this followed.


Nancy Houghtby mentioned we could bring graduation or birth pictures to figure out who is who as a fun game.


This is an election year! Nancy Schilling nominated Mary Mertinke for another two year term. Mary Lou Rockman seconded it. Mary graciously accepted.


Lisa Schilling willingly accepted the position of secretary for two more years.


Kay Lamb is our treasurer “until we ask her to retire”.


Judy Martino reported on the scholarship fund. The recipient of the Helen and Oscar Memorial Scholarship this year was Jennifer Gliniecki.


Judy also reported that Maria and Steve Hudson will be adopting a baby boy from South Carolina and will pick him up in August. Please pray for them as they give of themselves in this wonderful undertaking.


The Brilowski’s have a new baby granddaughter named Samantha.


Kay Lamb thanked everyone for their prayers for Alan Rockman and his family in the loss of their young daughter Callie. Kay asked for continued prayers for the family.


Nancy Houghtby reported on her dad’s health. Al will be moving soon to an assisted living complex. Al has had a series of small strokes and has some dementia going on.


Nicole Rockman introduced her friend, Drea from Stevens Point.


A round of applause was given to Diane Michalski for the wonderful job she does taking care of the cemetery plots that belong to our loved ones.


Mary is going to be looking to update contact information, when you send to her please let her know if you want to be listed or unlisted. Listed means that other members of the family can be provided your contact information if requested.

Suggestion was made to have a cork board with the meeting minutes, treasures report, scholarship fund and other information on it so people can view and read.


Sandy Sachman made a motion to adjourn the meeting. Lynn DeSalvo seconded the motion.
